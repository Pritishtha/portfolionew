Herquity — My Aspiration

I aspire to build Herquity, a finance + consulting platform for women entrepreneurs.

  • Core Idea: Finance + consulting for women founders (early-stage → scaling).
  • Problem: Women lack access to funding, mentorship, networks. Idea → execution gap is huge.
  • What We Do: Idea validation, financial planning, investor connect, strategic consulting.
  • Target Users: Aspiring women founders, early-stage startups, small women-led businesses.
  • Revenue Model: Consulting fees, success fee on funding, partnerships/workshops.
  • Differentiation: Finance-first + women-focused. Not just community → execution + capital access.
  • Inspiration: Platforms like HerKey focus on careers, not entrepreneurship + funding.
  • Plan: MBA → finance depth; 5–7 yrs industry exposure → launch Herquity.
  • Vision: Build a full ecosystem (incubator + IB-lite) for women founders.
  • Edge: Finance + communication (poetry, content, MC) with emotional connect.

👉 Herquity = “Goldman Sachs + startup incubator, but built only for women founders.”
